Tofu tagine

Source:

Serving size:

Ingredients

  • Firm tofu
  • Green olives
  • Lemon slices, with peel
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cilantro leaves
  • Ground coriander
  • Paprika
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Cayenne
  • Cumin
  • Ras el hanout
  • Raisings
  • Apricots
  • Tomato paste
  • Slivered almonds

Instructions

Coat tofu in spice rub, then braise everything together

Serving suggestion

Serve on top of couscous
