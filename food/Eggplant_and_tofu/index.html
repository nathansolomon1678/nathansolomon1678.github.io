Eggplant and tofu

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Firm tofu
  • Vegetable oil
  • White vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili garlic sauce
  • Garlic
  • Szechuan peppercorns
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Cornstarch (optional)

Instructions

Cut eggplant and tofu into 1-inch cubes. Toss in everything else. Boil and stir until it has a nice consistency. Serve with sticky rice. Note that if you add in cornstarch, remember to mix it with a liquid (probably soy sauce) first, or it will form lumps.
