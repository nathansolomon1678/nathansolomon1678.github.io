Mapo tofu

Serving size: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • Szechuan peppercorns, ground up
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • A tiny bit of coriander
  • Whole dried red chilies
  • Laoganma chili oil or chili sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Silken or soft tofu
  • Soy sauce
  • Fermented black bean paste
  • A small amount of corn or potato starch, dissolved in water
  • Green onions

Serving suggestion

Serve with white rice
