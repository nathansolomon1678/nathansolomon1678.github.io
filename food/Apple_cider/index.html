Apple cider

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of apple juice
  • A large orange, sliced, with the peel still on
  • One stick of cinnamon
  • A teaspoon or so of cloves

Instructions

Toss everything in a pot and boil it.

Serving suggestion

Serve either hot or cold, or anywhere in between. Strain out the solid parts, but save the orange slices (including the peel) to be snacked on later.
