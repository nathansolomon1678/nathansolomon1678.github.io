Mushroom and leek pasta

Pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta or gnocchi
  • Brown mushrooms
  • Leeks (or asparagus, but I prefer leeks)
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Miso paste or salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning (the dried herb mix)
  • Smoked paprika

Instructions
