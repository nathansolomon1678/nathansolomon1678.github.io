Apple streusel muffins

Source: based on https://www.bettycrocker.com/recipes/dishes/muffin-recipes

Serving size: A dozen muffins

Homemade apple streusel muffins in silicone muffin cups

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 granny smith apple, cut into small chunks

Streusel

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

Grease muffin tins, toss in batter, sprinkle streusel on top, and bake at 400Â°F for about 20 minutes, until golden brown. Immediately loosen from pan.
