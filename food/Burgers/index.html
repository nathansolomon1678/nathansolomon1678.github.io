Burgers

Ingredients

  • Beyond burgers (or Impossible burgers or whatever -- I like Beyond burgers though)
  • Cremini mushrooms, sauteed with a tiny bit of butter (and maybe parsley?)
  • Swiss cheese
  • Burger buns, lightly toasted

Instructions

Thaw patties in a microwave (assuming you buy them frozen), then cook them on one side for around 5 minutes, flip and throw cheese on top, and cook for another 5 minutes or so. Slap that on your bun with any other condiments and eat while still hot.
