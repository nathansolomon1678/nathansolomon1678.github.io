Massaman curry

Source: based on https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massaman_curry

Ingredients

  • Golden potatoes
  • Tempeh
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Chilies
  • Oil
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Nutmeg
  • Galanga
  • Coconut milk
  • Orange juice
  • Tamarind paste
  • Water
  • Bay leaves
  • Wild lime leaves
  • Lemongrass
  • Anise
  • Salt
  • Crushed peanuts

Instructions

Stew everything for a while, until the potatoes become as soft as butter and the broth becomes a thick stew. Serve with sticky rice.
