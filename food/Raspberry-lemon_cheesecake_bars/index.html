Raspberry-lemon cheesecake bars

Source: based on https://www.myfoodandfamily.com/recipe/207145/raspberry-lemon-cheesecake-bars

Serving size: Makes a 9" by 13" dish of cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Another 2 tbsp sugar
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 3 cups (12 ounces) raspberries (or any berries, really)
  • At least 2 tbsp lemon juice, plus some lemon zest if you feel like it
  • 2 pounds cream cheese
  • 4 eggs

Instructions

Line a 9" by 13" baking dish with aluminum foil. Mix graham cracker crumbs with the butter and the 2 tbsp sugar, then press that mixture into the baking dish and bake for 10 minutes at 325Â°F. Beat together the cream cheese, lemon juice (and zest), and sugar until smooth, then add the eggs and beat until mixture is smooth again. Mix in the berries, then toss that whole mixture over the crust, and bake at the same temperature for 3 to 40 minutes (until the cheesecake is set, and possibly cracking). Cool for a few hours before eating.

Serving suggestion

If you want, add some extra berries and zest on top.
