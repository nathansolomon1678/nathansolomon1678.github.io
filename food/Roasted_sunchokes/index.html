Roasted sunchokes

Source: https://thishealthytable.com/blog/roasted-sunchokes/

Ingredients

  • Sunchokes (aka Jerusalem artichokes)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Thyme
  • Lemon juice, honey, garlic, and other herbs (all optional)

Instructions

Cut sunchokes into half-centimeter-thick pieces. Toss all the ingredients together, then bake them at 400F for 20 minutes. Stir, then bake for another 15 minutes.

Serving suggestion

Eat this as a side dish, or mix it into a Greek salad.

Notes

Sunchokes are delicious, but they contain inulin, which can cause digestive problems. So don't eat to much, or you'll feel it the next day.
