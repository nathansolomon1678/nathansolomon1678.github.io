Blueberry cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • More than 2 cups of blueberries

Instructions

Grease 9" diameter pie dish. Mix dry ingredients & wet ingredients, separately, then combine. Then toss in butter. Sprinkle a spoonful of extra sugar on top. Bake at 350F for 35 minutes or so, until a chopstick comes out clean. Cool before serving.
