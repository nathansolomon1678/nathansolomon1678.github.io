Sticky rice with mangoes

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice
  • Coconut milk
  • A bit of sugar
  • A dash of salt
  • Mongoes

Instructions

Start by preparing sticky rice normally (soak for several hours, then steam for 25 minutes, flipping the steamer bag/basket halfway through).Then mix in coconut milk, sugar, and salt, and possibly cook for a few minutes? Serve with sliced mangoes on top.
