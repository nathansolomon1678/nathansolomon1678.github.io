# Sticky rice with mangoes
## Ingredients
* Glutinous rice
* Coconut milk
* A bit of sugar
* A dash of salt
* Mongoes

## How to prepare sticky rice normally
Soak rice for several hours, then steam for 25 minutes.

## Instructions
Prepare sticky rice, then mix in coconut milk, sugar, and salt, and possibly cook for a few minutes? Serve with sliced mangoes on top.
