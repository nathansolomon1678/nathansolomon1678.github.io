Sesame balls

Source: based on https://tasty.co/recipe/chinese-fried-sesame-balls

Serving size

You'll wanna make a 4x batch of this or it won't be worth your time

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 cups glutinous rice flour

Bean paste

  • 1/4 cups dried adzuki beans
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • enough water to soak, boil, and pastify the beans

Other stuff

  • Sesame seeds
  • Vegetable oil
