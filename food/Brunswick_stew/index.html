Brunswick stew

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic (not very much)
  • Okra
  • Tomatoes (canned tomatoes are fine)
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Liquid smoke
  • Unripe jackfruit
  • Tempeh
  • Lima beans
  • Green beans
  • Corn
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne powder

Instructions

Marinate tempeh and jackfruit in barbacue sauce, worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, salt, pepper, and cayenne. Caramelize onions in giant pot, then toss everything else in and cook until flavors are blended. Serve with cornbread.
