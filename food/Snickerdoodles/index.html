Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (salted) butter
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 3/2 cups sugar (+2 tbsp for coating)
  • 2 eggs
  • 11/4 (2+3/4) cups flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • More than 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Ground cardamom (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Mix wet ingredients & dry ingredients separately, then combine. Prepare cinnamon and sugar (and cardamom) mixutre. Roll dough into balls and roll those in the coating. Toss them on a baking sheet and bake until the edges are set but the centers are soft, roughly 8-10 minutes.
