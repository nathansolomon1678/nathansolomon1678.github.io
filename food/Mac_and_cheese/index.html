Mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Mustard powder (optional)
  • Smoked paprika (optional)
  • Cheddar cheese

Instructions

Cook and drain macaroni. Soften onions in roux, then add everything else, melting in the cheese last. Mix the pasta and cheese sauce, then bake that mixture until the top is darkened.
