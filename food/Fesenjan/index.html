Fesenjan

Source: based on https://www.feastingathome.com/fesenjan-recipe/

Ingredients

  • Walnuts
  • Pomegranate syrup / pomegranate molasses (or reduced pomegranate juice)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetable stock (optional)
  • Sugar (only add if the stew is sour)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Orange zest
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Parsley

Instructions

Toast walnuts so they aren't bitter, then blend them into a slightly coarse paste. Caramelize onion in olive oil, then add garlic, spices, and orange zest and cook for a while longer. Add the pomegranate syrup and the walnut paste, then simmer on low heat until done. Use very little turmeric, since that will turn it from nice purple to ugle yellow-green-brown.

Serving suggestion

Don't mess up, cuz pomegranate syrup is expensive.
